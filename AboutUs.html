ABOUT US

UniCafe:

Is a family-owned coffee farm located in La Union, Comayagua, Honduras. We have been growing coffee for over 15 years, and we are passionate about producing the highest quality coffee possible. Our coffee is grown at a high altitude, which gives it a unique flavor profile. We also use a 100% natural process, which means that our coffee is not chemically treated. This results in a cup of coffee that is clean, smooth, and full of flavor.

At UniCafe, we believe that coffee is more than just a drink. It is a way of life. We take pride in our coffee, and we are committed to providing our customers with the best possible experience. We offer a variety of coffees to choose from, including roasted beans, ground coffee, and more. We also offer a variety of coffee brewing equipment, so you can make the perfect cup of coffee at home.

We are committed to sustainability and environmental protection. We use organic farming methods, and we compost our coffee waste. We also support local communities by providing jobs and sustainability.

We invite you to try our coffee and experience the difference that UniCafe makes. We are confident that you will love our coffee as much as we do.
